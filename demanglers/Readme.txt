Sources of these C++ symbol demangler implementations:

demangle.h, cp-demangle.h and cp-demangle.c come from GCC's libiberty at http://gcc.gnu.org/svn/gcc/trunk/

These files are a mixture of LGPL and GPL + link combination exemption (see their preambles).


undname.c came from the Wine project at http://cvs.winehq.org/cvsweb/~checkout~/wine/dlls/msvcrt/undname.c

undname.c is LGPL licensed (see its preamble).
